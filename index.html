Jitin Jami

Doctoral Researcher at Machine Learning and Data Analytics Lab at FAU, Erlangen.
Former Master Thesis student at Institute of Computing (CI) at USI, Lugano.

I’m Jitin, a Doctoral Researcher at FAU’s Machine Learning and Data Analytics Lab in Erlangen since October 2023. I work in the Digital Biosignals Group headed by Dr. Heike Leutheuser . I’m actively involved in the SWODDYS project, a collaboration with Uniklinikum Regenburg and PreSens GmbH. The project is funded by Deutsche Forschungsgemeinschaft .
I was a double degree graduate student in Computational Engineering at FAU, Erlangen-Nurnberg and Computational Science at USI, Lugano. I have recently succesfully defended my master thesis (found here) supervised by Dr. Juraj Kardos , Prof. Dr. Olaf Schenk and Prof. Dr. Harald Köstler .
